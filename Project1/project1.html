Where is Azad Kashmir and Why it is Famous?

Azad Kashmir is a region located in northern Pakistan. It is renowned for its stunning landscapes, lush green valleys, majestic mountains, and beautiful rivers. Often referred to as the 'Paradise on Earth', Azad Kashmir attracts tourists with its serene environment and breathtaking views.

Activities to do in Azad Kashmir

  • Hiking and Trekking: Explore Kashmir's trails and enjoy the astonishing views.
  • Camping: Set up a camp by the riverside and feel the nature closely.
  • Fishing: Enjoy fishing in the clear waters of Kashmiri rivers and lakes.
  • Photography: Capture the beauty of nature in Azad Kashmir with your camera.

Important Locations in Azad Kashmir

  • Neelum Valley Valley famous for its lush greenery, scenic beauty, clear Neelum river, and enchanting streams.
  • Muzaffarabad: The capital city, known for its beauty and historical significance.
  • Rawalakot: Often called the 'Pearl Valley', known for its beautiful green hills and pleasant weather.
  • Banjosa Lake: A beautiful lake surrounded by pine forests, perfect for picnics and boating.
  • Ratti Gali Lake: An astonishing alpine glacial lake with breathtaking scenery.
